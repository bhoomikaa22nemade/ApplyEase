Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How do I get started with ApplyEase?

Simply register for an account, log in, upload your resume, and you're ready to analyze your resume, search jobs, and manage your applications.

What is the ATS Resume Checker?

The ATS Resume Checker evaluates your resume and provides an ATS score, identifies strengths and weaknesses, and suggests improvements to help increase your chances of getting shortlisted.

Can I search jobs based on my skills?

Yes. You can search jobs using your preferred role and location, then explore details like company, salary, required skills, experience, and job description.

How does the automation feature work?

Select the jobs you are interested in and start automation. ApplyEase tracks the selected jobs and keeps your application history organized for easy monitoring.

Can I track my applications?

Yes. The My Applications section helps you keep track of all selected jobs along with their application status and report.
